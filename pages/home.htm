Welcome to Oli

Our drawing studio offers drawing and painting classes in addition to pencil drawing lessons, academic drawing and painting classes, drawing with epoxy resin, drawing with alcohol ink, Chinese painting, master classes in oriental watercolour techniques, as well as courses in oil and watercolour painting. At our school every student has an individual group assignment. We pay individual attention to each mini group visitor.

Our teachers, teaching programmes and materials for painting and drawing are our strengths. All materials, brushes, paints, palettes, staging, easels, everything you need is provided by our painting courses. Teachers of painting courses undergo initial selection and training. Also, the Artacademy painting school offers drawing lessons for children of different age groups from 3-6, 7-9, 10-16.
